D6 Free Kriegspiel Solo Roleplaying

Setup
    List, track & maintain the following:

  • Themes (Campaign, Adventures, Scenes, etc.)
  • Characters (Remember that everything is a 'character'!)
  • Threads / Rumors
  • Maps (Campaign, Adventures, Scenes, etc.)
New Scene Format
    Roll 1d6:

  • 1-3 Action
  • 4-5 Quiet
  • 6 Meanwhile...
Set the Scene
  • Create a title.
  • List themes / situational aspects / senses.
  • Create a map and/or create zones.
  • Note the time, date, visibility, weather / atmosphere, etc.
  • Set the challenge
    • Enemies (Threats, Hitters, Bosses, Fillers, etc.)
    • Obstacles (Hazards, Blocks, Distractions, etc.)
    • Constrains (Countdowns, Limitations, Restrictions, etc.)
Taking Action
    Prepare Dice
  • Set Outcome A: Describe what 'success' looks like.
  • Approach (Careful, Clever, Flashy, Forceful, Quick, Sneaky, etc.)
  • Set Outcome B: Describe what you're afraid will happen if you fail or lose your nerve.
  • Time: Determine how long the action will take.
  • Establish Risk: impossible; demands a cost, extra steps, or specific item(s); or presents a risk. (Only roll to avoid risk.)
  • Determine Advantage / Disadvantage:
    • If acting with advantage (characteristic, situation, etc.) roll 2d6 and keep the highest result.
    • Else, if acting with disadvantage (hindered, injured, burdened, etc.) roll 2d6 and keep the lowest result.
  • Accept Help: If a character uses their turn to help, they roll. You take the highest overall result. Your ally shares any consequences.

    • Roll Dice
    6 Outcome A amplified!
    5 Outcome A as expected.
    4 Outcome A with a twist.
    3 Outcome B with a twist.
    2 Outcome B as expected.
    1 Outcome B amplified!

    Twist Check
    A twist occurs on a roll of 1 on a 1d6.

    Twist Focus (1d6)
    • 5-6 Character / Creature
    • 3-4 Environment
    • 1-2 Item or Theme

    Twist Action (1d6)
    • 4-6 ...helps the hero
    • 1-3 ...hinders the hero
Optional Random Encounters
    When time passes, roll for a random encounter.
Asking Questions, Perceiving Facts
  1. Envision two options (eg: [A or B], [Yes or No], [Strong or Weak], etc.)

  2. Determine Modifiers

    • Likely Yes / A: 2d6, keep highest
    • Likely No / B: 2d6, keep lowest

  3. Roll Dice

    4. 6. Yes, and... / A amplified!
    5. Yes, as expected. / A as expected.
    4. Yes, but... / A with a twist.
    3. No, but... / B with a twist.
    2. No, as expected. / B as expected.
    1. No, and... / B amplified!


Now, Play On!
